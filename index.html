Music History of Shiosan

I love music. I have listened to really cool music since I was a teenager.
Here is my music history.

I started getting into music when I was 12-13 years old. That's when I listend to Beatles.

Then I got into Western music, so I made more friends who loved music and started listening to many kinds of Western music. But at that time, there was a little information. I only see MTV or read foreign music magazine which was rare at that time.
In that situation, we only had two ways to go. we either follow the trend or get wierd. At first, I follow the trend too.
Bon Jovi, Motley Crue.

But things have changed since Guns N' Roses came on the scene.

After they showed up, I started looking at every bit of information they had. Then I got to know their roots and the music they grew up listening to.
Hanoi Rocks, Aerosmith, Kiss, and etc.
As an aside, I remember Axel Rose said that he was told gospel music as a child.

In addition, I got to look into the roots of these musicians. In the process, I got into the 70s and 80s.

Chuck Berry, Rolling Stones, Sex Pistols...

That's when things started to get crazy. As I followed the roots, and then the roots again. I got hooked on a wide range of music from the 60's to the 90's.
Johnny Thuders, New York Dolls, Ramones, Janis Joplin, Muddy Waters, Bob Dylan, and etc.

Now that I think about it, I was a mucky high school student, but at the same time, I am proud of it.
